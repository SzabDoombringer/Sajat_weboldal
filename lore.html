The Guardian of Balance

It is said that when the universe of Roblox was still young, MrDoomBringer descended from the stars. His presence brought order where chaos reigned. With one hand, he could bestow treasures upon mortals; with the other, he could summon doom upon the unworthy.

The Great Trials

Legends claim that MrDoomBringer would walk among mortals, hidden in plain sight, to test their loyalty. Those who proved virtuous found their names remembered; those who strayed were cast into eternal obscurity, struck from the divine records of Robloxia.

Fan lore written by the community, dedicated to the Guardian of Balance.
